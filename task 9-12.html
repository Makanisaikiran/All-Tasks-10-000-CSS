Making Tea

  1. Pour fresh water into a kettle and bring it to a boil.
  2. Place a tea bag or loose tea leaves into a teapot or cup.
  3. Pour the hot water over the tea bag or leaves. Let it steep for 3-5 minutes, depending on how strong you like your tea.
  4. Remove the tea bag or strain the leaves. Add milk, sugar, or lemon to taste, if desired, and enjoy your tea!

Making of Coffee

  1. Heat water in a kettle until it reaches the boiling point. If using a coffee maker, follow the manufacturer's instructions.
  2. Add coffee grounds to a filter in your coffee maker, French press, or pour-over setup. Use about 1-2 tablespoons of coffee per cup of water.
  3. Pour the hot water over the coffee grounds. Let it brew for 3-5 minutes. If using a coffee maker, simply start the brewing process..
  4. Once brewed, pour the coffee into a cup. Add milk, sugar, or cream to taste, if desired, and enjoy your coffee

Making Chicken Noodles Soup

  1. Chop 1-2 carrots, 1-2 celery stalks, and 1 onion. Mince 2-3 garlic cloves. Cut 1-2 chicken breasts or thighs into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Heat some oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onions, carrots, and celery. Cook for 5-7 minutes until the vegetables start to soften. Add the minced garlic and cook for another 1-2 minutes.
  3. Add the chicken pieces to the pot. Cook until the chicken is no longer pink, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Pour in 6-8 cups of chicken broth. Add salt, pepper, and herbs like thyme, bay leaves, or parsley to taste. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Add 1-2 cups of egg noodles to the simmering soup. Cook according to the package instructions, usually about 6-8 minutes, until the noodles are tender.
  6. Remove the bay leaves and adjust the seasoning if needed. Serve the soup hot, garnished with fresh parsley if desired, and enjoy!
